Common questions

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No — your 7-day trial starts the moment you create an account. No payment info required until you decide to subscribe.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What's the difference between monthly and pay-per-activity?
If you create more than 4 activities per month, the $8 subscription pays for itself. Pay-per-activity is great for teachers who want to try one activity before committing.
Do activities work offline?
Yes — every downloaded activity is a self-contained HTML file. Once opened in Chrome it works completely offline. Perfect for Chromebooks in schools with spotty WiFi.
What subjects are supported?
Currently math — operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division) and algebra (one-step and two-step equations). More subjects are on the way.
